Welcome to my blog!

The only important information you really need to know about me is that I grew up in the beautiful Meteora. The rest is self-promotion :)

I have over 10 years of experience as an economist and in macroeconomic research, mainly working in academic and policy-making and economic research institutes. During this time, I developed strong knowledge of the US and UK economies, and to a lesser extent the Eurozone. Academically, I specialize in fiscal policy, macroeconomic theory, computational economics, and to a lesser extent asset pricing and financial theory.

Outside academia, I also had the opportunity to work as a data scientist, which gave me a good understanding of the machine learning ecosystem and its applications. At heart and by training, I remain a macroeconomist, and I am fortunate to have developed a strong quantitative and mathematical background during my years of study and research. This has allowed me to approach problems with a great deal of flexibility but also made me a kind of polymath across different disciplines. My (unusual) career path is a reflection of this.

Friends would describe me as thoughtful. That is because they have see me enjoying debunking economic theories and ideas with solid academic theory or my own reflections and predictions.

I intend to share my knowledge and curiosity about macroeconomic and financial issues in this blog. I will draw on my experiences and knowledge from academia, policy making, and portfolio management (trading) to write about these topics. I may also post other topics of general interest.

I’m always happy to connect with new people. Feel free to message me on social media.

I hope you enjoy the content here.
